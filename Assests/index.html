Why Spokane? Spokane's motto "Near nature, near perfect" says it all... well, almost. There are also a lot of great things happening in web design here in Spokane that make it an exciting market for what we do. 14Four and Seven2 have been bringing big interactive web design contracts with national brands to Spokane for years, and turning out some really innovative work. There are specialty SEO firms like Ed Reese of Sixth Man Marketing and great production firms like Zipline Interactive, Design Spike and 20 Miles North that turn out a lot of great sites. Not to mention several ad agencies in the area that have really strong web teams as well, including folks like BHW1, KlundtHosmer, and MDI Marketing.

Spokane also has a really active social media community and our own online networking group, LaunchPad INW. There's also a great collection of talented photographers and traditional artists around to collaborate with. With all this, Spokane is really establishing itself in the web/design world.

If you are looking to establish your own unique web presence or take your web design to the next level, take a look around and shoot us a line to get started on your web design today.
